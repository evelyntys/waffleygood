About Us

Our Story

Waffley Good Company is a little cafe tucked away on a beautiful old bridge in the historical city of Durham. Launched in 2015 out of our passion to cook perfect waffles in a sociable setting, the cafe’s reputation for delicious waffles, both sweet and savoury, has attracted waffle-lovers from far and wide.

The Mission

Our mission is to make sure that every waffle from our extensive menu is cooked with both love and skill for the pleasure of our customers. Great waffles, superb coffee and good company – what could be better?
